# Product: YouTobia Multimedia

YouTobia is an Ethiopian multimedia company platform (based in Addis Ababa) with a branded web presence that acts as a digital hub for its suite of products and content.

## Core Sections
- **Hero** — fullscreen video splash with animated brand loader
- **Story Scroll Journey** — scroll-driven narrative about the brand
- **Brand Ecosystem** — showcases YouTobia's product lineup
- **EnqoqCash** — interactive trivia/quiz game with virtual ETB prize rewards; the main gamification product
- **Staff Hierarchy** — org chart showing departments and team members
- **Media Hub** — blog and vlog content feed with likes
- **Footer** — navigation + social links

## Brand Identity
- Primary color: `#FF1E27` (high-octane red)
- Palette: red + deep black (`neutral-950`) + absolute white
- No purple gradients, no rounded "glassy" cards — sharp corners, strong grids
- Typography: serif italic for headings, mono for UI/system labels
- Tone: architectural, precise, high-contrast

## Key Vocabulary
- "EnqoqCash" / "Enqoq" — the trivia product (from Amharic "enqoqlsh")
- "YouTobia" / "YUTOBIA" — brand name (used interchangeably)
- Virtual prizes denominated in ETB (Ethiopian Birr)
